our services

Digitalization of medical reports

It is designed to help save time, boost accuracy and efficiency, and combine technologies in ways that are new to healthcare with paper less technology.

Lung pneumonia prediction

Lung detection and severity prediction of pneumonia patients is based on the range of symptoms a person is suffering from within a couple of days like chest pain, cough fever nausea or shortness of breath.

Heart disease detection

Various tests are used to diagnose heart disease by taking your personal and family medical history, recording current and past symptoms, and doing laboratory tests and an electrocardiogram.

Bone fracture detection

X-ray or CT scan reports provide the insight of a particular bone to be examined and diagnosed and we treat accordingly based on the severity of the fractured part.

Depression Prediction

We have also created a Depression Detection section, where the user will be asked 3 major questions. Those 3 questions will be designed in such a way that when the user will write the answer to those questions, we will detect that the person is suffering from any kind of anxiety or depression. If not, then also we will update you that the person is totally fine, and he is not suffering from any kind of depression. If he is suffering, he should take some therapy sessions, either by our prescribed doctor or any other doctor which suits the patient.

Doctor Recommendation

At last, we have added a list of our personally recommended doctors. The user will be able to get all the necessary details of the recommended doctors, with the doctor's name, address, and user rating. There will be a list of doctors from that particular place so that the patient will be able to take an appointment with a doctor who has a good rating, or can select the doctor, which is closest to him.

about us

"We are in this Together!"

Meditrack is an online health information resource for patients, their families and friends. We aim to eliminate the chaos of physical reports and records by digitalizing medical records. We offer a simple and easy-to-use platform for people to store all their medical data and keep track of their medical expenses and bills.

We also use advanced disease prediction detection to help identify potential health risks and provide our patients with the best care possible.
